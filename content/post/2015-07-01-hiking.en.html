This week we used the excellent weather conditions for a group hike.

After we had figured out where actually to go,

… and some involved grass scrambling

… we finally reached our “summit”, where we took a well-deserved break

… and marveled the gorgeous view.

“What goes up must come down” and so we eventually returned back and cooled down with a beer or two,

… replenished the burnt calories,

… after which we needed some help for digestion,

… before we closed the hike with a game of viking chess.
