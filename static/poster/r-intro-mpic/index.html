What is this

This is an anouncement of a 4.5-hour course for:

  • beginners without progamming background,
  • students and earliy career scientists who are interested in R, and
  • senior scientists who are interesed in reproducible research.

This poster was created via R. It is reproducible, accessful online, and interactive (try the demo data table below). Wanna know how? Come and join us!

When and where

  • Seminar Room on the first floor at Max Planck Institute for Chemistry.
  • Tuesday (4th Dec. 2018) afternoon and Wednesday (5th Dec.) morning.

How to participate

  • This course is devided into four parts (see the headings in this post). Feel free to take part in any one or all of them.
  • Send a registration email (mentioning which part you would participate) to peng.zhao@mpic.de by Monday, 3 Dec., 2018.
  • Bring a laptop for live practice. Although examples are give on Windows, other OS will be compatible. R and RStudio must be installed in advance. See below.
